Location: System DWR43
Date: 201.5.29

Fringe survey fleet received information suggesting that there exists an inactive coronal hypershunt in Sector DWR43. The Company decides to dispatch an elimination fleet to destroy the roaming Remnant forces around the hypershunt, and that task falls to you, commander.


There are something off about these autonomous warships, they cleansed the population of DWR43X without instruction. To ensure success, the company will dispatch the most resource intensive flagship for this operation.


You can hold F and re-select this mission to restrict unusable equipments. If you want to restore the original setting, please select this mission again without holding F. Please note, the restriction will affect everything. If you want to remove the restriction, you must select this mission without holding down any buttons.
